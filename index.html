About The Event

ICCAP-2022 is an upcoming conference aimed exclusively at evidence-based clinical practices in audiology and communication disorders. We believe that this conference will be beneficial to researchers, professionals and students as it brings together a panel of highly accomplished experts across the globe in the field of communication disorders.

AIM

The International Conference on Communication Disorders and Audiological Practices (ICCAP) is dedicated towards bringing together leading academicians, researchers, professionals and students from the field of communication disorders to discuss innovative ideas, exchange information and disseminate knowledge on novel issues and breakthroughs in evidence-based clinical practice.

Objectives

  • To provide a solid platform to share novel ideas through seminars and scientific paper presentations.
  • To provide an opportunity to enhance and share skills through discussions.

Desired outcome

At the end of the conference, the participants would be able to

  • Develop a multidimensional perspective on cleft lip and palate.
  • Have integrative insights on neuromotor disorders.
  • Have a better understanding of aural habilitation based on the ICF-CY framework.
  • Recognise challenges in clinical audiological practises.
  • Augment knowledge on the identification and management of sensory issues, and management of problematic sexual behaviours in individuals with ASD.
  • Have a deeper grasp on the intervention of neurogenic language disorders in adults.

F.A.Q

  • Why do I have to pay to attend?

    The process of planning and implementing an international conference with thousands of attendees and the top experts in the world costs money. NISH is nonprofit organization and in order to put on a conference, we require funds to do so. Registration fees help offset the costs associated with organizing and hosting an event of this scale. We have surveyed similar conferences and our registration fees are relatively modest compared with other large international conferences and for the level of the program being offered.

  • How do you register for ICCAP?

    For registration plese click the registration tab in the menu. Then choose which category you would like to register (Student/Professional). First "make payment" from the portal and then register by clicking the registration tab.

  • Do I need any addiotional document for registering as student?

    Yes, You need to upload the bonafide certificate duly signed by your HOD while registration if you are registering in the student category.

  • Is it possible for me to attend the conference online?

    Yes, the ICCAP is indeed conducting in hybrid mode. Zoom will be the online platform. You can use the same registration form and choose "mode of attendance" as "online" if you want to attend the conference online. Online participants pay a different rate. Before paying, please refer to the tariff on the registration page.

  • How do I register for CRE points?

    Participants must attend the conference in offline mode on all 3 days in order to get CRE points. Please check the box next to Professionals (with CRE points) on the registration form. Please be aware that CRE points are only available to the first 150 registered participants.

  • Is it possible to register for just one day?

    Yes, you can register in both online and offline modes for one, two, or three days. Single-day registration has a different tariff. Please go to the registration page for the tariff rates.
